Type of Scholarship
Family
Sunbic is giving you up to 15% discount for your family member that studies in our universities except the one who already graduated . And if you got one of your family member teaching for at least 3 years at Sunbic Universities around the world or made contribution with our society such as research and others that fits to our standard
Score
Sunbic is giving you up to 75% discount until your last semester of your college if you got any kind of achievement in sport, academics, or anything else or with a report card that fits to Sunbic Standard, and while you studied in college if you got any achievement such as perfect grade or become a winner that brings Sunbic University name
Test
Sunbic is giving you up to 100% discount until your last semester of your college if you have done some extra tests in our university around the world that fits to our standard. And if you got full scholarship, you will work under Sunbic coorporation for another 3 years, between this 3 years you are not allowed to work at the other companies.
Economy
Sunbic is giving you up to 50% discount until your last semester of your college and all of your primary needs such as place for living, food, insurance, university books. All of this things can be achieved if you fits to our standards and you need to upload some document and you will got another test to achieve this type of scholarship
